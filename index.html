WHAT WE OFFER


KisanKraft is a manufacturer, designer, importer and distributor of affordable farm equipment suited to the needs of small and marginal farmers. We have more than 300 models of small machineries, covering all major operations of cultivation. Our products are approved by FMTTI and empanelled by most states under subsidy programs for farm mechanization.
